About me

Dr. Michalis Smyrlis possesses extensive experience and expertise in cybersecurity, with a particular focus on software design and development. He holds a BSc from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Crete and a PhD in Computer Science with a specialization in Cybersecurity from City, University of London . As part of his doctoral research, he developed and implemented an innovative hybrid approach for risk assessment in cyberspace systems. With a strong background in applied research, Dr. Smyrlis has contributed to more than 30 European research projects, many of which he coordinated from a technical standpoint. He currently serves as the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at Sphynx, where he leads the company’s technological vision and strategic direction. In this role, he is responsible for architecting and guiding the development of advanced cybersecurity solutions, ensuring software quality, and aligning engineering initiatives with business goals. He oversees the full product lifecycle—from design and implementation to delivery—while fostering innovation and maintaining high standards in software security and performance. In addition to his industry role, Dr. Smyrlis is a Scientific Collaborator at the European University of Cyprus. There, he teaches courses and supervises postgraduate research within the Master’s programme in Cybersecurity.
