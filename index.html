About me

Dr. Michalis Smyrlis possesses extensive experience and expertise in cybersecurity, with a particular focus on software design and development. He holds a BSc from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Crete and a PhD in Computer Science with a specialization in Cybersecurity from City, University of London . As part of his doctoral research, he developed and implemented an innovative hybrid approach for risk assessment in cyberspace systems. He has participated in more than 30 European research projects, serving as Technical Coordinator in several of them . Currently, Dr. Smyrlis holds the position of Chief Software Engineer at Sphynx , where he leads technical guidance, defines the overall software architecture and design direction, and ensures the successful delivery of high-quality software products in the field of cybersecurity. Additionally, Dr. Smyrlis is a Scientific Collaborator at the European University of Cyprus. Within the framework of the university's Master's programme in Cybersecurity, he teaches courses and supervises postgraduate theses.
